About Sua Pa AI

Sua Pa AI is Ghana's first AI-powered educational assistant designed specifically for the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum.

Our Mission

To democratize quality education across Ghana by providing every student with a personalized AI learning companion that understands their needs and supports their educational journey.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Sua Pa AI was born from the vision of making quality education accessible to every Ghanaian student. We recognized that students often struggle with homework, lack personalized support, and need tools that understand the local curriculum. Sua Pa AI bridges this gap.

What Makes Us Different

  • GES Curriculum Aligned: Built specifically for Ghanaian students
  • Affordable: Quality education shouldn't break the bank
  • 24/7 Available: Learn anytime, anywhere
  • Personalized: Adapts to your learning style
